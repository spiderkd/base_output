## Design notes

Three key details make this feel like a riso component:

**The hard shadow.** A secondary-color rectangle sits `translate(3px, 3px)` behind the track with zero blur. The track appears to float above the paper on a thin ink pad.

**The halftone unfilled area.** When `variant="halftone"`, the empty portion of the track shows a radial-gradient dot pattern at 18% opacity — like the paper is visible through a coarse screen.

**The ink bleed at the leading edge.** Where the filled area meets unfilled, a 16px gradient bleeds from primary into the overlap color. This simulates ink spreading beyond the sharp edge of a riso plate — the fill literally "bleeds" at its boundary.
