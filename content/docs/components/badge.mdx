## Design notes

The **overprint** variant renders two rectangles with `mix-blend-mode: multiply`. The overlap region produces the "third ink" color automatically — just like physical riso printing where two ink layers create a new color where they cross.

The **stamp** variant uses `border-style: dashed` to simulate a rubber stamp impression.

That restraint is what keeps `Badge` feeling printed instead of purely digital: the offsets stay small, the edges stay sharp, and the ink contrast does most of the visual work.
